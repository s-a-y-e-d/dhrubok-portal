এই ব্লুপ্রিন্টে ধ্রুবকের বর্তমান শিক্ষক নম্বর এন্ট্রি, মালিকের পর্যালোচনা, অপরিবর্তনীয় প্রকাশনা, সংস্করণভিত্তিক সংশোধন, প্রিন্টযোগ্য রিপোর্ট ও অভিভাবক SMS-এর সঙ্গে নমনীয় ব্যাচ নির্বাচন, CQ + MCQ নম্বর বণ্টন, বিষয়ভিত্তিক ফলাফল এবং স্বয়ংক্রিয় মেধাতালিকা যুক্ত করা হয়েছে।

06 / MERIT

Dynamic course-based merit list

The merit list is calculated automatically from the complete, reviewed result set—but becomes official only when the owner publishes it.

Primary: higher grand total
Tie-break 1: higher total CQ/written marks
Tie-break 2: higher total MCQ marks
Still tied: same merit position
Do not rank by “obtained marks” and “total marks” as separate valuesAll candidates in one merit scope should be evaluated against the same configured full marks. Ranking uses obtained grand total; percentage is useful when valid candidates can have different totals.

08 / REPORTS

Reports for students, teachers, and owners

Every report is bilingual, printable, version-labelled, and based on the frozen published snapshot.

Individual result

Subject marks, components, grand total, percentage, pass/fail, merit, comments, publication version, and print date.

Tabulation sheet

A4 landscape view of every candidate and subject, including total, result, and official merit position.

Merit list

Course-wide official ranking with optional batch filters for internal analysis.

Subject analysis

Highest, lowest, average, pass percentage, absence count, score distribution, and batch comparison.

Student history

Results across exams, subject trends, failure history, and attendance shown as context—not a mark adjustment.

Correction history

Previous and new values, reason, editor, approving owner, publication versions, and corrected-SMS status.
